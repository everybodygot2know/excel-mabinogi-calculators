# Mabinogi Excel Calculators
A comprehensive Excel-based toolkit I built during my Mabinogi (Nexon) days to help players make smarter gear, skill, and stat decisions!
You simply plug in your character's stats and other values — and it instantly shows you "which option is better", DPS comparisons, damage breakdowns, and more.

Included Calculator(s):

* **Protection Logarithm** - helps players figure out the stat ratio of "Protection" and "Magic Protection" to their respective "Actual" damage reductions & HP increases.

How to Use:

1. Download the .xlsx file
2. Open it in Microsoft Excel (recommended: Excel 2016+ or Microsoft 365)
3. Fill in the highlighted cells with your character stats and other values
4. Compare the options and make decisions based on the results 

Note: Some content may be outdated due to game updates, but the core calculations and formulas remain valid and useful!
